About NetLink

A professional alumni networking platform.

How Alumni Help Students

Alumni play an important role in guiding students toward successful careers. Through NetLink, alumni mentor students, share real industry experiences, and provide career guidance. Alumni also help students by posting job and internship opportunities, conducting mock interviews, and offering professional advice.

By connecting students with experienced professionals, NetLink reduces the gap between academics and industry and helps students build confidence, skills, and strong professional networks.
